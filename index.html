Privacy Policy - Global Meditate

Effective Date: 05 October 2025

Your privacy matters to us. Here's what we collect and how we protect you:

Location Data

  • We request your approximate location (country/region level only)
  • Used solely to show you on the global meditation map
  • You can hide your location from the map at any time
  • Precise GPS coordinates are never stored or shared
  • Location sharing is completely optional

Other Data We Collect

  • Anonymous meditation session participation
  • App usage statistics (anonymous)
  • Temporary reactions and energy waves on the map
  • No personal information, emails, or names

How We Use Data

  • Display real-time participant counts
  • Show global meditation community on world map
  • Enable sharing positive energy and reactions
  • Improve app performance
  • Create a sense of global community

Your Control

  • Toggle map visibility on/off anytime
  • Location permission can be revoked in device settings
  • All map data is temporary and auto-deleted
  • No account required - completely anonymous

Data Sharing

We never sell or share your data with third parties. Location data is only used within the app to connect you with the global meditation community.

Data Storage

  • Meditation sessions: Anonymous, temporary
  • Location data: Country level only, can be hidden
  • Reactions: Auto-deleted after 5 seconds
  • No permanent user profiles created
